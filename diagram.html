Diagram


This diagram indicates the process that we have designed for the users to use the website. First of all, after the user has clicked on our website, he should choose what kind of user he is, then he could register an account according to his preference. After that, the user need to log in to our system, if successful then he will be directed to next stage, if not then the details he entered might be wrong and he will need to go back and register a different account. After the user has logged on, the user could enter the search details depends whether he would like to search for SMEs or Credit providers. Finally, the results after searching will be displayed, and the user could contact them to complete the process.
